About Me

I love solving problems using design as a tool. What drives me is my desire to improve the lives of users through meaningful designs & interactions and in the process push myself to be a better version of what I was yesterday.

From my experience in working at a small startup environment to a large design team of a successful travel company and doing master’s in an interdisciplanry HCI course, I have gained a unique set of skills in product design, programing and problem solving.

I spend my spare time traveling, learning classical music, experimenting with new recipes, reading books on design & science fiction or taking a good nap.
