Introduction

HealthBox is a system of connected devices that works to monitor, measure & manage health and fitness. The system includes a smart band, a digital weighing scale & a heart rate monitor device, all centrally connected to UA’s Record app. The goal of our project is to conduct usability testing on the platform and report the findings to HTC.

The tool was adjudged one of the 'Best Innovation of the year' during an internal-Ericsson 'Innovation Week' event.

Investigation

During our first conversation with HTC, we were briefly explained about the idea, motivation and the functionality of the HealthBox system. We got a chance to interact with the researcher and the industrial designer who worked on this product and gained several insights.

Defining The Scope

While we were free to choose what aspect of the connected system we wanted to conduct usability studies on, we scoped our study down to two aspects:
  • The initial onboarding and setup process of the entire system.
  • The usability of the UA Band’s fitness features.
